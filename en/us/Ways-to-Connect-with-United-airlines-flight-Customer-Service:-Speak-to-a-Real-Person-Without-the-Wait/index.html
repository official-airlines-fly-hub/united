Contact a Live Agent at United Airlines™

How to Reach a Live Agent

To speak directly to a live agent, call: 1-800-265-0080 (US/OTA).

After automated prompts, say "agent" or press "0" to connect faster.

Why Speak to a Live Person?

  • Flight changes or cancellations
  • Booking clarification
  • Refunds and compensation
  • Technical glitches during check-in or booking

Contact Methods

  • Phone: 1-800-265-0080 or say “agent”
  • Live Chat: Visit the United Airlines™ website Help section
  • Mobile App: Use the Fly United™ app for live chat and support
  • Email: Ideal for non-urgent and documented queries
  • Social Media: Message on Twitter or Facebook

Step-by-Step: Speak to an Agent

  1. Dial 1-800-265-0080
  2. Follow prompts for your issue
  3. Press “0” or say “agent” to skip to a person
  4. Tip: Call during off-peak hours for shorter wait times

International Support Numbers

  • USA: 1-800-265-0080
  • Canada: 1-800-265-0080
  • Australia: 1-800-265-0080
  • Spanish Support: 1-800-265-0080

Common Customer Service Topics

  • Flight changes and cancellations
  • Hotel booking problems
  • Refunds, travel credits, and disruptions
  • Duplicate boarding passes or seat upgrades
  • Business-class upgrade inquiries
